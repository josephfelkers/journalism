BIOGRAPHY

Joseph Felkers is a senior at Catholic Central High School in Grand Rapids, Michigan. At CC, he acts as the Editor-in-Chief of their student run newspaper, the Blue and White. Outside of journalism, he remains involved in the humanities as the captian of the debate team, delegate in the Model United Nations team, and as a nationally recognized poet.

(LEFT) CHCEKING IT TWICE: Checking the printed copies of their January issue, co-Editors-in-Chief (L-R) Joseph Felkers ('18) and Joslyn Fox ('17) ensure that there are no print errors before distribution. After a previous error that resulted in a reprint, the Blue and White staff carefully proofreads digital galleys before sending them to print.
